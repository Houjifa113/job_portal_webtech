About the job:

As a System Analyst, you will evaluate, design, and implement IT systems to support business processes. You will collaborate with stakeholders to understand requirements, analyze workflows, and recommend improvements to software and hardware systems.

You will be responsible for documenting functional and technical specifications, assisting in testing new systems, and ensuring seamless integration with existing IT infrastructure. Your work will help optimize operations, improve system efficiency, and support strategic decision-making.

The role involves close coordination with software developers, project managers, and IT teams to ensure systems meet business needs and are scalable, secure, and maintainable.

The US base salary range for this full-time position is $110,000-$160,000 + bonus + equity + benefits. Salary varies based on experience, skills, and location.

Responsibilities:

  • Analyze and evaluate current IT systems and business processes.
  • Gather and document technical and functional requirements.
  • Collaborate with developers to design system solutions.
  • Test and validate new systems, identifying and resolving issues.
  • Provide guidance and support to end-users and IT teams.
  • Maintain up-to-date documentation and system specifications.
