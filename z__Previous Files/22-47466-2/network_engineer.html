Network Engineer

Summary:

Age: 27 to 40 years

Experience: At least 3 years

Salary: Negotiable

Minimum qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.
  • 2+ years of experience in network administration or engineering.
  • Strong understanding of TCP/IP, routing, switching, and firewalls.
  • Hands-on experience with network monitoring and troubleshooting tools.

Preferred qualifications:

  • Master's degree in Networking, IT, or a related field.
  • Industry certifications such as CCNA, CCNP, or CompTIA Network+.
  • Experience with cloud networking (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Knowledge of network security practices and tools (VPNs, IDS/IPS, firewalls).

About the job:

As a Network Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining scalable and secure network infrastructure. You will ensure high availability, reliability, and performance of network systems that support business operations.

You will work with cross-functional IT teams, troubleshoot network issues, and provide long-term solutions to enhance connectivity and security. Your work will directly contribute to maintaining seamless communication across global offices, data centers, and cloud services.

This role also includes planning and executing network upgrades, ensuring compliance with industry standards, and documenting network processes and configurations.

The US base salary range for this full-time position is $95,000–$140,000 + bonus + benefits, depending on skills, certifications, and location.

Responsibilities:

  • Design, configure, and maintain LAN, WAN, and wireless networks.
  • Monitor network performance and troubleshoot issues proactively.
  • Implement security measures such as firewalls, VPNs, and encryption.
  • Collaborate with IT teams to support servers, applications, and cloud environments.
  • Document network configurations, changes, and troubleshooting steps.
  • Stay updated with emerging networking technologies and provide recommendations for improvements.
