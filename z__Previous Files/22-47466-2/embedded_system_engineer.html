Embedded System Engineer

Summary:

Age: 28 to 40 years

Experience: At least 4 years

Salary: Negotiable

Minimum qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or related field.
  • 2+ years of experience in embedded software or firmware development.
  • Strong knowledge of C/C++ programming for embedded systems.
  • Experience with microcontrollers, real-time operating systems (RTOS), and hardware-software integration.

Preferred qualifications:

  • Master's degree in Embedded Systems, Electrical Engineering, or Computer Engineering.
  • Experience with ARM Cortex processors, Linux kernel, or FPGA-based systems.
  • Familiarity with communication protocols (I2C, SPI, UART, CAN, Ethernet).
  • Knowledge of debugging tools, oscilloscopes, and hardware test equipment.

About the job:

As an Embedded System Engineer, you will design, develop, and optimize software and hardware solutions for embedded devices. You will work on low-level programming, driver development, and real-time applications that run on constrained hardware platforms.

Your role includes developing system architecture, ensuring reliable hardware-software integration, and collaborating with cross-functional teams including hardware engineers, product designers, and QA teams.

You will also play a key role in testing and debugging embedded systems, ensuring they meet performance, safety, and reliability standards across a wide range of industries including automotive, IoT, robotics, and consumer electronics.
