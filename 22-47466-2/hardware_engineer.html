About the job:

Hardware Engineers at our company design, develop, and test computer hardware, including circuits, PCBs, processors, and embedded systems. You will work with cross-functional teams to ensure hardware meets product specifications, performance targets, and quality standards.

In this role, you will manage hardware design projects, coordinate testing procedures, and troubleshoot issues from prototype to production. The position requires strong analytical skills, creativity, and the ability to work with other engineering teams.

Our hardware products power next-generation technologies, from computing devices to advanced embedded systems. You will contribute to innovation in hardware design and help bring high-quality products to market.

The US base salary range for this full-time position is $120,000-$180,000 + bonus + equity + benefits. Salary depends on experience, skills, and location.

Responsibilities:

  • Design, test, and implement computer hardware and embedded systems.
  • Develop schematics, PCB layouts, and prototypes.
  • Collaborate with software engineers and other teams to ensure hardware/software integration.
  • Debug hardware issues, perform root cause analysis, and implement solutions.
  • Maintain documentation for hardware designs, revisions, and testing results.
