Cybersecurity Engineer

Summary:

Age: 25 to 40 years

Experience: At least 3 years

Salary: Negotiable

Minimum qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Cybersecurity, Information Security, or a related field.
  • 2+ years of hands-on experience in cybersecurity engineering or IT security roles.
  • Knowledge of firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems (IDS/IPS), and endpoint security solutions.
  • Experience with vulnerability assessment and penetration testing tools.
  • Strong understanding of security protocols, encryption, and authentication mechanisms.

Preferred qualifications:

  • Master's degree in Cybersecurity, Information Assurance, or a related field.
  • Industry certifications such as CISSP, CEH, CISM, CompTIA Security+, or OSCP.
  • Experience with cloud security (AWS, Azure, or GCP).
  • Familiarity with SIEM tools (Splunk, QRadar, etc.).
  • Strong incident response and digital forensics skills.

About the job:

As a Cybersecurity Engineer, you will be responsible for protecting organizational networks, systems, and data from cyber threats. You will design and implement security measures, monitor systems for suspicious activity, and respond to security incidents.

You will work closely with IT teams to ensure compliance with security policies, perform vulnerability scans, and recommend improvements to strengthen defenses. This role also requires staying updated on the latest security trends and technologies to anticipate emerging threats.
