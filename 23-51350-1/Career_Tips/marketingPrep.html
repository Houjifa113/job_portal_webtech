Marketing Prep Tips
Understand core marketing concepts (4Ps, segmentation, targeting, positioning)
Familiarize yourself with digital marketing channels (SEO, SEM, social media, email)
Learn to use marketing tools (Google Analytics, HubSpot, Mailchimp)
Study consumer behavior and market research techniques
Develop strong communication and copywriting skills
Stay updated on marketing trends and best practices
Build a portfolio with sample campaigns or projects
Practice analyzing marketing data and metrics
Network with marketing professionals and join relevant communities
Prepare for common marketing interview questions
