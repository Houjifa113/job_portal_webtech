Salary Negotiation Techniques
Research industry salary benchmarks for your role and location
Assess your skills, experience, and unique value to the employer
Practice your negotiation pitch and responses to counteroffers
Be prepared to discuss your total compensation (salary, benefits, bonuses, etc.)
Delay salary discussions until you receive a job offer
Express enthusiasm for the role while negotiating
Use data and examples to justify your salary expectations
Be polite, professional, and confident throughout the process
Consider non-monetary benefits and perks in your negotiation
Be willing to walk away if the offer does not meet your minimum requirements
